Who we Are?

Tours in Mauritania is a company located in Mauritania. We provide travellers to Mauritania with lists of attractions and adventures such as riding on the iron or train, The longest train in the world, camel trekking, tours to the four ancient world heritage cities, the eye of Sahara, desert excursions and more. We do solo tours, group tours and customized tours. We help you see the real Mauritania and get acquainted with its culture and traditions. With us, you will see Mauritania like never before.
