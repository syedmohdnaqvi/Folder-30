About Us

At Tours in Mauritania, we love showing people the hidden wonders of one of Africa’s most special and untouched places. Our goal is to give you amazing travel experiences that show the unique beauty, rich culture, and old traditions of Mauritania.

Who We Are?

Tours in Mauritania is a company based in Mauritania, dedicated to providing travelers with unforgettable experiences. We offer a wide range of attractions and adventures, including riding on the iron ore train—the longest train in the world—camel trekking, and tours to the four ancient World Heritage cities. Explore the Eye of the Sahara, enjoy desert excursions, and much more with us.

Our Mission

We want to be the best travel company in Mauritania. We are known for our great service, deep local knowledge, and our promise to be kind to the environment. We want to connect our guests with the rich culture of Mauritania and help them understand and appreciate it through great travel experiences.

We offers!

We offer solo tours, group tours, and customized tours tailored to your interests. Our aim is to help you see the real Mauritania and get acquainted with its rich culture and traditions. With us, you’ll experience Mauritania like never before.
